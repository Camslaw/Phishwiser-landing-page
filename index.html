How does it work?

Email

You provide us the emails you would like to use for testing. Our application can send out a batch of phishing emails to a specified group of people. These emails have multiple templates.

Track

The application will keep track of data tied to the recipient. This will include what kind of email worked, how many times, at what time, etc.

Train

In the future we will implement a training feature. After the recipient clicks on the phishing link, a page will open telling them that they have been phished, the dangers of phishing and how to avoid it.

Analyze

We provide an interface for you to see the results of the testing. These can be used to analyze your organization's susceptibility to phishing attacks and make important decisions.

Is it worth using?

In the first quarter of 2022 the Anti-Phishing Working Group (APWG) observed 1,025,968 total phishing attacks. This is the first time the quarterly total has exceeded one million. While phishing attacks are a threat that many people are aware of, not everyone is able to identify one, or know how to train their employees on how to handle them.
